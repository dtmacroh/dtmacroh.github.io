Visualizing Crime in Calgary and Inter-Alberta Municipalities

Description

This project was meant to vizualize crime in Calgary, and other municipalities within Alberta, Canada. I chose to do this project with the initial intent to see if Forest Lawn, a community in Calgary that I live in, is safe. Since then, I have refocused my intentions to have a more exploratory approach of the data set. Here are the questions I am most interested in answering:

  • Are older residential communities more prone to crime in Calgary (when it was built, sectors in the city)> Does it differ depending on the type of crime?
  • What crime is the most prevalent in each city? Are there any differences in crime between municipalities?
  • What is the ratio of adult vs youth charged with committing crimes, on average? Does it change drastically depending on the crime?
  • What are the top 2 crimes in Alberta that are strongly correlated with one another?
  • Is there a month or year that crime rises in Calgary? (e.g. before Christmas, after New Yearâ€™s where debt is high, summer where the weather is good, recessions)
